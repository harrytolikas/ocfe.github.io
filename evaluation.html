OSINT CTI selected:

Attribute Meaning Evaluation Level Comment
Timeliness The speed and promptness with which new and updated information about potential cyber threats is delivered to the recipient, sharing and receiving up-to-date information in a timely fashion.
Relevance The extent to which the information provided in the feed is applicable to an organization’s specific needs and risk profile.
Completeness The extent to which the information provided in the feed is comprehensive and covers all relevant aspects of a potential cyber threat.
Ingestibility The ease with which the information provided in the feed can be consumed, processed, and integrated into an organization’s existing security infrastructure.
Accuracy The degree of correctness and reliability of the information provided in the feed.
Uniqueness Refers to the distinctiveness and originality of the information provided in the feed.
Actionable Refers to the extent to which the information provided can be used to take concrete, practical steps to defend against potential threats.
Compliance The extent to which the feed aligns with legal and regulatory requirements that govern the collection, use, and dissemination of cyber threat intelligence.
Intelligence Refers to the information and insights provided by the feed that can be used to improve an organization’s security posture and defend against cyber threats.
Predictability The degree to which the feed can provide a forecast or estimate of future cyber threats and attacks.
Maintenance Refers to the effort required to keep the information in the feed up-to-date and relevant.
Priority Refers to the level of importance or urgency assigned to the information provided in the feed.
Impact Refers to the level of harm or damage that a potential threat could cause to an organization’s systems and networks.
Utility of Products The practical usefulness and value that the information provided by the feed delivers to the user or organization.
Context Refers to the background information or additional details that accompany the information provided in the feed.
False Positives Refers to the number of incorrect or misleading information provided in the feed.



Type of CTI Meaning
Tactical Detailed information about specific threats or attacks, such as the tools and techniques used by attackers and their targets.
Strategic Long-term trends and threats, providing decision-makers with insights into potential threats and risks to their organization. It includes information about the threat actors, their motives, and tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs).
Technical Technical details related to a threat, such as vulnerabilities, exploits, malware, and infrastructure used by the adversaries.
Operational Information that is actionable for security operations teams, such as indicators of compromise (IOCs) and TTPs that can be used to detect and respond to threats.
